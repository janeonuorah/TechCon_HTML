History

TechCon began as a small developer meetup in 2010 and has since evolved into a premier global conference, uniting tech enthusiasts, engineers, and innovators from all over the world.

Mission

Our mission is to foster a community of passionate technologists by promoting knowledge sharing, innovation, and collaboration through inclusive and engaging conferences.
